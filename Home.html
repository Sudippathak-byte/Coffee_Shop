Nicaraguan Honey Process coffee features a unique production method where mucilage is left on beans during drying, resulting in a sweet, fruity flavor profile with bright acidity.

Ethiopian Light Roast coffee is celebrated for its vibrant acidity and complex flavors. It often features fruity notes like berry and citrus, complemented by floral aromas and a tea-like body.

Blue Mountain coffee is renowned for its mild flavor, smooth body, and bright acidity. Grown in the Blue Mountains, it has a distinct floral aroma and nutty undertones.
